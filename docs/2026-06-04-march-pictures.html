March photos

June 04, 2026

Here's a collection of some of best photos from March. We were in New Zealand in March and headed over to Australia at the end.

Notable is that were present for the Blood Moon and golly do I wish I'd had a tripod for that. Holding a camera with 2kg 200-800mm lens up at the right angle to capture the moon and keep it steady for a slow shutter speed... is hard. Most of my pictures of it are blurry.
