Gotta love English ISP's

Cost of cancelling our broadband:

  • 20 pounds cancellation fee (which they just added a month ago)
  • 14.99 pounds hook up fee which is waived after 12 months, we haven't quite been with them for 12 months
  • 19.99 pounds for last months service

So ~55 pounds. Thank you plus.net, Virgin was terrible too. Golly as much as Rogers suck, I didn't realise how good they were in comparison. All ISP's and Telco's suck, it's one of those universal rules. Some just suck more than others.
